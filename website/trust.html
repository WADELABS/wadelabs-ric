RIC is built for defensive assurance: authorization-only execution, evidence-backed claims, and audit-ready reporting.

AUTHORIZED TARGETS ONLY

RIC runs only against environments you are authorized to test (typically staging/preview). No scanning of third-party systems without explicit permission.

EVIDENCE FOR EVERY CLAIM

RIC does not make claims without artifacts. Traces, network logs, and assertion outputs are attached. No evidence = Unknown.

DEFENSIVE BY DESIGN

RIC validates integrity guarantees and regression drift. It does not provide exploit tooling or step-by-step abuse instructions. The goal is prevention: catch integrity decay before production.

FAQ

Is this a vulnerability scanner?

No. RIC focuses on workflow invariants and system integrity gaps that surface under real user behavior and release drift.

Does RIC map to standards?

Yes. Findings can map to OWASP ASVS and NIST 800-53 for audit alignment.

What do you need from us?

A target URL, authorization confirmation, and (if needed) test credentials in a scoped staging environment.
