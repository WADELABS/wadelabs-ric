HOW IT WORKS

RELEASE INTEGRITY PIPELINE

01

You provide an authorized staging or preview environment.

02

RIC executes protocol-style workflows across auth, billing, exports, and role boundaries.

03

Evidence is captured automatically (Playwright traces, network logs, assertion output).

04

Findings are classified as System Integrity Gaps (not “bugs”).

05

Drift compares runs to detect regressions introduced by releases.

RIC is designed for defensive assurance. Authorization-only. Evidence-only.

EVIDENCE-ONLY RULE

RIC does not report theoretical risk. Every finding must include:

  • > Trace evidence
  • > Network artifacts
  • > Assertion logs
  • > Reproducible workflow proof

NO EVIDENCE = UNKNOWN

MINIMAL OVERHEAD

No meetings required. You receive:

  • > Integrity Snapshot report
  • > Evidence links for every claim
  • > Drift summary (NEW / FIXED / UNCHANGED)
  • > Escalation only for critical integrity breaks

SCOPE, BY DESIGN

RIC focuses on invariants that fail under pressure: session state, authorization boundaries, idempotency, exports, and sensitive change workflows.

Want a classic pentest? Use a pentest. Want release-time integrity proof with drift detection? Use RIC.
