MOST SECURITY FAILURES AREN’T VULNERABILITIES

Teams ship fast. Systems drift quietly. The most expensive failures aren’t exploits — they’re integrity breaks:

  • > Sessions that remain active after logout
  • > Role boundaries that erode over time
  • > Billing actions that double-apply under retries
  • > Exports that leak across tenants
  • > Sensitive changes without re-authentication

These issues rarely appear in code review or scanners. They appear after releases — in real usage.
